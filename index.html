typetest

A Google Font tester for web pages

Quick testing of Google Font inside the actual page. Built out of necessity and for convenience when trying to prototype designs and choosing between webfonts.

Use:

  1. inlcude typetest.min.js in your page.
  2. initiate by either
    1. calling typetest(); on a click event - or -
    2. calling typetest(); on document.ready - or -
    3. calling typetest(); via console (recommended)
  3. click the load button to set a new class of body{ font-family: 'Your font'}

Example URL's (copy & paste into typetest)

  • http://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Open+Sans
  • http://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Bree+Serif
  • http://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Source+Code+Pro
